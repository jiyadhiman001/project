A bonafide superstar of the sport and the TATA IPL, Virat Kohli has been with the Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB) through thick and thin. IPL Profile
Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer who plays for the RCB franchise in the Indian Premier League. Virat Kohli was born on Nov 05, 1988; and as of 2025, he is 37 years old. Virat Kohli is a batter from Delhi and bats right handed. He made his IPL debut in 2008. Virat Kohli has played 262 matches to date in his IPL career and has scored 8,447 runs, with an average of 39.47.
captaincy record
The team's longest-serving captain is Virat Kohli, who led the side in 143 matches between 2011 and 2023. RCB reached the final once (2016) under his leadership and advanced to the playoffs four times (2015, 2016, 2020, 2021).
